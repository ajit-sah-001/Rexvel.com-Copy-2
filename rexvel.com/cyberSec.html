🔐 CyberSecurity Overview
What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of defending digital systems, networks, and data from cyber threats such as hacking, malware, and data breaches. It plays a vital role in protecting information and infrastructure globally.

Key Fields
  • 🧠 Ethical Hacking & Pen Testing
  • 🛡️ Network & Endpoint Security
  • ☁️ Cloud Security
  • 🔍 Threat Intelligence & Analysis
  • ⚙️ Security Operations (SOC)
Top Certifications
  • 📜 CEH (Certified Ethical Hacker)
  • 📜 CompTIA Security+
  • 📜 CISSP (Security Professional)
  • 📜 OSCP (Offensive Security)
